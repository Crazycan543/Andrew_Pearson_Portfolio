Gantt Chart Creator

The primary purpose of the Gantt Chart Tiny Tool is to allow users to easily create a visual chart to guide and assist with scheduling activities in a timeline format. The goals are: - To create a professional-looking chart that can be used in projects and presentations - To minimize the effort to reduce the time and cost of creating a Gantt chart - To provide an easy-to-use platform to create Gantt charts for project scheduling
