Blaze Smarthome Fire Alarm

The main purpose of Blaze is to provide a smart solution for fire alarms. Currently, fire alarms once triggered are unable to be manually disabled, so when you have a false alarm caused by steam from showering for example, you would be unable to disable the alarm. Blaze addresses this issue by allowing the users to confirm the existence of a fire when the alarm is triggered. The main goals of Blaze are: ● To send a notification to a user device when their fire alarm is triggered ● To allow the user to temporarily disable the alarm in the case of a false alarm ● To notify any other users of an active fire alarm in the case that it is confirmed to be a fire ● To enable the rest of the fire alarms when the fire has been confirmed or if it isn’t confirmed within a timeout period
