Song Share

When your friend sends you a song and you forget to listen to it, it can negatively impact your friendship. This app will ensure that you won’t lose those recommended songs. Have you ever been sent a song recommendation? Have you ever forgotten it the next day? Of course you have, because we all have (unless you don’t have friends). This application will solve this issue by allowing people to recommend you songs on the app. Along with recommendations, you will be able to reply, ”listened”, and give feedback based on what you thought of their song choice. Never miss a song someone recommends to you again, “listen at your own beat.” This app allows you to send music recommendations directly to your friends. The app will store your recommendations so that you can easily keep track of what music you’ve sent and what music has been sent to you, on a person by person basis. After listening, marking the song as “listened” will add it to the archive, and allow you to send a note with your thoughts.
